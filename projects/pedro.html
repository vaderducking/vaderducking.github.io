Pedro

After I was gifted a fitbit versa 3 smartwatch and not being able to find a Pedro clockface, I decided to make my own. You can see it published on the fitbit store by clinking on this link.


I had to learn how to simulate an animation with HTML SVG. This works by changing the "href" attribute of the image tag within JavaScript functions that select each frame approprietly. Then once I had Pedro jumping around, I added the time in white font on the middle of the screen.


I finished by integrating the KPay SDK for people to be able to purchase the clockface.
